Slides

Purpose

Slides are the core for displaying content. Each slide is what the customer will see displayed on your digital sign.

Usage

You can create a new slide from the page you are currently on. You will choose some a set of pre-defined templates in order to arrange your content. Once you select a template you can click on the empty template sections to upload pictures that you want to display to your customers.

Tips

If none of our templates fit your needs, you can always use photo-editing software to create your own slide, with your own template and then use our 1 image template to display whatever you desire.
